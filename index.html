Our Story

Why the first bite lingers

The Tanjore Tiffin Room was built for people who want South Indian food to arrive with heat, depth, texture, and occasion. The room is polished, but the reason people come is simple: the flavours land hard and stay with you.

Chettinad spice, Kerala coconut curries, Konkan seafood, ghee-finished roasts, and bar pairings that sharpen every bite. Nothing here is museum-piece tradition. It is food meant to be torn into, shared loudly, and remembered on the way home.

This is where guests come when they want the smoky ghee roast, the bright seafood curry, the late-night biryani, and the drink that makes them order one more dish.
